About

This notebook featuring a gorgeous peacock design is a twist on the classic composition book used by school children in Canadian elementary schools.

All notebooks are made from completely recycled materials. The 160 plain pages of unbleached white paper are made from 100% post-consumer waste in blue with an adjacent margin in red, and are micro-perforated for easier and neat page removal. The covers are heavy card and the energy used in production is bio-gas, with a spiral bind for even easier note-taking! The inside cover features all kinds of witty, useful information and sketches.

All notebooks are made in Canada. As the name suggests they are less about what they were and what they are, and more about what they can become!
